Steak Club Leidse:
This place is a great grilling restaurant where great flavors meet unforgettable nights! It has options of steaks, seafood, and even vegan options.

Amigo Grill Restaurant:
This is a great Argentinian restaurant in Amsterdam! There are steaks, Wines, Dulce de Leche, Mate, and other options.

Bello Gio's By The River:
This restaurant is a stylish and welcoming destination, with its cuisine, atmosphere, and music.

Humphrey's Amsterdam:
This is a great restaurant, the setting is beautiful, the service is fast and helpful. The dishes are a great experience, sometimes even giving too much food. Great for leftovers!

Moon:
This is an experience like no other, it is a revolving restaurant, so it gives a 360 degree view of Amsterdam and its stunning surroundings. The menu is always changing to fit customer wants and seasons, giving you a fresh perspective every visit.
