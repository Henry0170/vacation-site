AMSTERDAM WEATHER
Summers in Amsterdam are usually warm with occasional colder periods, so you may want to remember a coat, even if you think you probably won't need it.
Winters in Amsterdam are usually fairly cold with rain, wind, and some snow. Occasionally, temperatures can dip to below freezing during the winter time.
