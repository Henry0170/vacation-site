Rijksmuseum:
This is a museum of the Netherlands, it tells the story of the Netherlands from the middle ages to the 20th century. This museum includes works by Rembrandt, Vermeer, Frans Hals, and more!

Van Gogh Museum:
Discover the world's largest collection of works by Vincent Van Gogh, featuring masterpieces such as Sunflowers, The Potato Eaters, and others.

The Amsterdam Dungeon:
This shows Amsterdam's murky past. It walks you through about 500 years of history in around 80 minutes. It's a great experience.

Vondelpark:
At one of Amsterdam's most famous parks, travelers can enjoy a host of outdoor activities amid beautiful English-style landscaping.

Electric Ladyland:
This museum shows off the art of fluorescent lights, offering a stunning visual of colors and shapes. A super cool experience, one worth seeing.
