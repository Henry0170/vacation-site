Day 1:
Visit Vondelpark, its the perfect spot to walk around, enjoy nature, or even rent a bike to explore the park’s beauty.

Day 2:
Go to the Van Gogh Museum, home to the largest collection of works by Vincent Van Gogh.

Day 3:
Get your adrenaline pumping with a visit to the A’dam Lookout. Experience panoramic views of Amsterdam and, if you're feeling daring, try the “Over the Edge” swing.

Day 4:
Visit Electric Ladyland, the first museum of fluorescent arts. It's a fascinating, one-of-a-kind experience.

Day 5:
Visit the Rembrandt House Museum, where the famous Dutch artist lived and worked. It’s a great way to get a glimpse of the 17th-century artist’s life.

Day 6:
Spend a leisurely morning at Vondelpark for a relaxing start to your day, then grab a bite to eat at the Amigo Grill Restaurant.

Day 7:
Revisit the A’dam Lookout for a second time, or maybe try the swing if you missed it earlier.
